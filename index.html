Rules

How to play:
  1. Press any key or click "Start" button to start game.
  2. Simon randomly selects a colored button. Now you must click that same button. Got that right? Great!
  3. Now, Simon will randomly select another colored button. You must remember and click the first button followed by the second.
  4. Simon will continue to select only one new random button in the sequence and add it to the end, while you must remember and click the entire sequence in order.
  5. Make it to 10 and you win! Hit a button wrong - game over!
